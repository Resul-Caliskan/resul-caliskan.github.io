Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Kabadayi Games ("we", "our", or "us") handles information in connection with our mobile game, Ballgorithm.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data automatically, except for the information required to run the game's online leaderboard features:

  • Usernames: When you register or log in within the game to use the leaderboard, you create a custom username. This username is stored in our database hosted on Supabase to display your rank to other players.
  • Scores: We store your high scores in our database to manage the global leaderboard rankings.
  • Authentication Credentials: We store a username and a hashed password locally and in the database to authenticate your device and securely update your high scores. We do not collect or store your real email address.

2. How We Use the Information

We use the collected usernames and high scores solely to operate, display, and maintain the global leaderboard in Ballgorithm. We do not use this data for marketing, advertising, profiling, or tracking across other apps and websites.

3. Third-Party Services

Our database backend is powered by Supabase. All authentication requests and score submissions are transmitted securely directly to our Supabase database instance. You can review Supabase's Privacy Policy at supabase.com/privacy.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your usernames, passwords, and scores from unauthorized access or alteration. Passwords are encrypted/hashed before storing. However, please remember that no transmission over the internet or mobile network is 100% secure.

5. Children's Privacy

Ballgorithm does not collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and realize your child has submitted a username that contains personal details, please contact us so we can delete or rename the entry.
